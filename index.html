A few questions you might have. 🙋

What if I don't have a lead magnet yet?
No problem, creating one is part of the service we're offering here. We'll learn about your services and audience, then work with you to build something that makes sense for your practice. If you already have one, we use it or can clean it up.
What email platform do I need?
You'll need a paid email platform that supports tagging, automations, and opt-in forms, like ConvertKit (Kit), Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Mailerlite, or Flodesk. Not sure if yours qualifies? Just ask before purchasing. If you don't have one yet, we can help you set it up.
Do I need a website?
A website is helpful for hosting your thank you page, and it's also where your emails will send people to learn more about your services – so it's definitely an important part of the funnel. If you don't have one however, reach out before purchasing and we'll figure out the best path. It's not always a dealbreaker.
How much will the ads cost me?
About $150/month at $5/day, paid directly to Meta. That's separate from the $1,297 you pay us. You own the ad account, control the budget, and can pause anytime.
What happens after 7 days of support?
The funnel keeps running without us. Most practitioners leave it running in the background with minimal maintenance. If you want continued support after that, we can talk options, but it's not required.
Will this work for my specific practice?
If you have a clear service you're offering and are ready to start growing your list and bringing in more leads, this will work for you. The funnel we build is designed to be simple, effective, and tailored to your specific audience and niche — not a generic template. If you're not sure, the 20-minute call is the perfect place to talk it through before committing.
Will there be any live calls involved?
We don't anticipate needing to get on any calls. All of the work is done asynchronously – we get started, you check this off your to-do list! Everything is communicated through our project tools and Slack, so there's no scheduling required on your end.
How much work will I need to do?
Not much. You can expect to spend about 30 minutes filling out our onboarding questionnaire up front, then roughly 30–60 minutes total over the course of the project to review what we've built, share feedback, and make any minor tweaks. If you don't have a lead magnet yet, expect to spend another 30-60 minutes reviewing the draft we create for you. Otherwise, we handle everything else!
How will you make sure everything sounds like me and matches my brand?
We collect the key details from you upfront using our intake tools – your voice, your values, your audience, and your style. Everything we create is built from that foundation. We base all copy, design, and messaging on your brand, not a generic template. You'll also have a chance to review and give feedback before anything goes live.
